Section 08

Booking Modifications

If you need to change your travel dates, passenger details, or other booking specifics — rather than cancelling — the following applies:

  • Date Changes: Subject to availability and fare difference. A modification fee of ₹200–500 per booking may apply in addition to any fare difference.
  • Name Corrections: Minor spelling corrections are free. Full name changes (different passenger) are treated as a cancellation and re-booking.
  • Adding Passengers: Subject to availability. New passengers are priced at current rates, which may differ from original booking price.
  • Upgrading Room/Seat: Subject to availability. Difference in cost is charged at prevailing rates.
  • International Bookings: Modifications may not be possible on certain international fares. Check with ZoomFly support before requesting changes.

To request a modification, contact our support team on WhatsApp at +91 8076136300 with your booking reference and the specific change required.

Section 09

Exceptions & Non-Refundable Scenarios

The following scenarios are not eligible for refunds under any circumstances:

  • Bookings explicitly marked as Non-Refundable at the time of purchase.
  • Cancellations made after the applicable free cancellation window has expired.
  • No-shows — failing to appear for a flight, hotel check-in, bus departure, or cab pickup without prior cancellation.
  • Denied boarding due to invalid or expired travel documents (passport, visa, government ID).
  • Failure to meet health requirements, entry regulations, or immigration rules at the destination.
  • Voluntary no-shows during natural disasters, strikes, or force majeure events (ZoomFly will assist with rebooking where possible).
  • Promotional or discounted bookings marked as non-refundable.
  • Partially utilised packages (e.g., hotel used but flight not taken — no partial refund for unused services).

🚫 ZoomFly is not responsible for losses arising from third-party travel disruptions, government restrictions, or personal emergencies. We strongly recommend purchasing travel insurance to cover such scenarios.

Compassionate Exceptions: In genuine medical emergencies or bereavement situations, ZoomFly may consider waiving cancellation fees at its sole discretion. Supporting documentation (medical certificate, death certificate) must be provided within 72 hours of cancellation request.
