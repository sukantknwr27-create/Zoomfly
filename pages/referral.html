Frequently Asked Questions

When do I receive my 500-point reward? +
As soon as your referred friend completes their first booking (minimum ₹5,000 transaction value) — the points land in your account within 24 hours, and you'll get a WhatsApp notification when they do.
Is there a limit on how many friends I can refer? +
No limit at all. Refer 10 friends, earn 5,000 points. Refer 100, earn 50,000 points. There's no cap on referral earnings.
How long does my referral link stay active? +
Your referral link never expires. Share it anytime and earn whenever someone books through it.
What counts as a qualifying booking? +
Any completed and paid booking on ZoomFly with a minimum value of ₹5,000 — flights, hotels, packages, buses, or cabs all qualify.
Can I refer someone who already has a ZoomFly account? +
No — the referral bonus is only awarded for new customers who sign up using your link and make their first booking.
Where do my earned points go? +
Points are added to your ZoomFly Loyalty balance (see My Rewards) and can be redeemed for discounts on any future booking. They're valid for 12 months from the date earned.
