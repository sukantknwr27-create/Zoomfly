FAQ

Common Questions

What is the minimum group size? +
The minimum group size for group booking rates is 10 travellers. For groups of 5–9, standard individual booking rates apply but we can still coordinate travel together.
How much advance do I need to pay? +
A 25% advance secures your booking and blocks all seats/rooms. The remaining 75% is due 30 days before departure. For last-minute bookings (under 30 days), full payment is required upfront.
Can individual travellers within a group be added or removed? +
Yes, subject to availability and the airline/hotel's group policy. Additions are welcomed anytime. Removals after 30 days of departure may incur cancellation charges as per our refund policy.
Do you arrange visas for international groups? +
We provide visa guidance and can arrange visa assistance for popular destinations like UAE, Singapore, Thailand, and Schengen countries. Visa fees are charged separately.
What if the group size increases after booking? +
Contact your dedicated coordinator as soon as possible. We'll check availability and add the additional travellers at the same group rate if space permits. This is handled on a best-effort basis.
